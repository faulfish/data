Checking for Updates

OmniDiskSweeper can automatically use your Internet connection (when active) to check for new and updated versions of itself. You can choose to check for updates manually or schedule automatic checks from the Preferences pane. Select Preferences... from the OmniDiskSweeper menu.
When Manually is selected you can check for updates by clicking the Check for Updates Now button. To schedule automatic checking select Automatically and then select how often you want to check for updates. You will be notified when there is a new version of OmniDiskSweeper ready for you to enjoy.
